Their names are [[Alynu Aralen]], [[Sathasa Nerothren]], [[Fothyna Herothran]], and [[Alveleg]]. They're hiding in a cave located somewhere on top of the rocky formation behind the [[Caldera]] Mine. When the four agents are eliminated, please [[report]] back to me. Take care; it's very likely that one of them is serving as a lookout posted outside the mines.
